# Health Tracker (Prototype)

A simple and intuitive health tracking application to help you monitor your daily wellness metrics.

**Prototype Status:** This is an early prototype. Features may be incomplete and data may be reset during updates. Please do not rely on this for critical health information.

## What It Does

Health Tracker lets you easily log and view your daily health metrics including weight, steps, sleep hours, and water intake. Track your progress over time and stay motivated to reach your wellness goals.

## Key Features

- **Easy logging** — Quickly add daily entries for weight, steps, sleep, and water
- **View history** — See your logged entries over time to track trends
- **Simple interface** — Clean, straightforward design for everyday use
- **Local storage** — Your data stays on your device (prototype limitation)

*(Additional features coming in future versions)*

## Screens & Flows

The app includes a main dashboard where you can:
- View your most recent entries at a glance
- Add new health entries through a simple form
- Navigate to your history to see past entries

## How to Use

### Add a New Entry

1. Tap the **Add Entry** button on the dashboard
2. Enter your health metrics (e.g., weight in lbs/kg, steps taken, sleep hours, water in glasses/ml)
3. Tap **Save** to store your entry

### View Your History

1. Tap the **History** tab or icon
2. Browse your past entries by date
3. Scroll to see older entries

### Edit or Delete an Entry

1. Go to the **History** section
2. Find the entry you want to change
3. Tap on the entry to view options
4. Select **Edit** to modify, or **Delete** to remove the entry

*(Note: Edit and delete features may not be available in all prototype versions)*

### Tips for Best Results

- **Be consistent** — Log your entries at the same time each day for better tracking
- **Use consistent units** — Stick to pounds or kilograms, not both in the same period
- **Set reminders** — (If supported) Enable notifications to remind you to log daily
- **Check trends** — Review your history periodically to see your progress

## Data & Privacy

### What Data Is Stored

This prototype stores your health entries including:
- Weight
- Steps
- Sleep hours
- Water intake
- Date/time of entry

### Where Data Is Stored

Currently, this prototype stores data locally on your device.

### Privacy Notice

- **This is a prototype** — Data handling may change in future versions
- **Do not enter sensitive medical information**
- Your data is not currently synced to any cloud service
- If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your logged entries may be lost

## Known Limitations

This is an early prototype with the following known limitations:

- Data may be reset during updates
- No cloud backup or sync across devices
- No account system — data is device-specific only
- Limited editing and deletion capabilities (varies by version)
- No data export features
- May not support all health data types yet

We are actively working on improvements and new features.
